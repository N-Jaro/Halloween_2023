String of Pearls (Senecio rowleyanus)

String of Pearls is a unique succulent with trailing stems and spherical, bead-like leaves, ideal for hanging baskets or containers.

Care Instructions

  • Light: Bright, indirect sunlight.
  • Water: Water sparingly and allow soil to dry between waterings.
  • Temperature: Prefers 70-80°F (21-27°C).
  • Fertilizer: Use diluted, balanced liquid fertilizer sparingly during the growing season.
  • Pruning: Trim long stems to encourage bushier growth.
  • Propagation: Propagate via stem cuttings in a well-draining succulent mix.

Additional Tips

  • Ensure good air circulation to prevent humidity buildup.
  • Repot every 2-3 years to facilitate growth.
  • Toxic if ingested; keep the plant out of reach of children and pets.
